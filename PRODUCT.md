# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary audience: mothers and families assembling a baby layette or choosing a personalized children's gift. They arrive on mobile or desktop while deciding whether this atelier feels trustworthy enough to engage — often comparing handmade care against generic store enxoval. Context is emotional and practical at once: a baptism, maternity, birthday, or first home textiles for a child.

## Product Purpose

Ateliê GM is a static brand site for a hand-embroidery atelier specializing in children's pieces and complementary home textiles (bed, table, bath). There is no checkout backend; the site's job is to show craftsmanship and catalog options so the visitor can choose a piece with clarity, then continue on WhatsApp when ready. Success is fewer idle curiosity chats: people who open WhatsApp already know what they want.

## Positioning

Every piece is hand-embroidered to order — not a generic store enxoval.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: choose a product in the catalog
- Secondary CTA: open WhatsApp for a quote even without a selection
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: Bordados artesanais feitos com carinho
- Belief ladder:
  1. The finish is carefully made
  2. It fits my moment (baby / home)
  3. A custom quote is worth asking for
- Proof on hand: product photography when available (place files in `.impeccable/assets/proof/`). No testimonials yet.

## Brand Personality

delicado · afetuoso · exclusivo

Voice is warm and precise, never corporate. The emotional goal is trust in handmade work — calm confidence that a real person will stitch the piece with care.

## Anti-references

Must not feel like a startup landing page: purple gradients, identical icon cards, SaaS marketing grammar, or generic “AI boutique” templates that could sell any product.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust before talk** — the visit proves craft and fit; WhatsApp is the continuation, not the only screen.
2. **Catalog as the primary path** — help her pick a piece so the chat starts specific.
3. **Show the stitch** — photography and physical detail carry belief; decoration without craft is empty.
4. **One clear claim** — hand-embroidered to order, every time.
5. **Never SaaS costume** — reject startup landing patterns that erase the atelier.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prioritize solid contrast, full keyboard access, and respect for `prefers-reduced-motion`. No formal WCAG certification target yet; keep these three as the floor on every pass.
